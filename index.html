Grandparents Clothes Service Project

Elderly Clothing Project for the people in Convalescent homes at Willow Glen. Helped by Sathya Sai Baba Center of Central San Jose

This is a project undertaken by the students of Sai Spiritual Education (SSE) to contribute new clothes for our Grand Parents located at the Willow Senior Center in San Jose. We invite you to participate in this noble endeavor and provide your encouragement to all of our us.

Participate

Our Goals

We invite you to participate in this noble service that SSE is undertaking for our Willow Glen Senior Center grandparents by signing up for providing new sets of sweatshirt and pants [as a set]. These sets can be the sizes L, XL, and XXL. Our goal is to collect 135 pairs of sweatshirts and pants (45L, 45XL, 45XXL). Preferred colors are green, gray, black, and navy blue.

About/FAQ

We are students of a Spiritual Educational group called SSE. We aspire to follow the teachings of Sathya Sai Baba, which foster the values of truth, right conduct, peace, love, and non-violence.

Can I ask Others to Help?

Ofcourse!! You can tell as many people as you want.
This service is inclusive and will allow anyone who seeks to do so to help out. Give the link http://region7saicenters.org/gpcs/ to them

Can I buy something extravagant or different from the recommended?

I would recommend that you should not buy something that is different from the guidelines. This is because we don't want to make one grandparent feel different than the other.

Can the clothes have Stripes?

We would not recommend it. However, it's okay if that's the only clothes you find.

Whom do I give to?

You can give this in the Drop Off Locations or Give it to Kamesh Uncle, Geetha Aunty, etc.

Where do I give?

You can drop off the clothes at Geetha Aunty's House, Pavan uncle's house and Ramanj uncle's house. There are also drop-offs in Center and in SSE.

How many can I Give?

Can I give used Clothes?

No.

What is the Last Date to Drop off?

April 1st at the center will be the last date to drop off

What are the sizes am I supposed to give?

The Sizes are S,M,L,XL,XXL.We are giving 45 large, 45 extra large, and 45 extra extra large.

What colors can I buy?

The colors that you can buy are red,blue,green,and brown.
